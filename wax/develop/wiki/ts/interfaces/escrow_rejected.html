Interface escrow_rejected

Related to escrow_approve_operation and escrow_transfer_operation. Generated when pending escrow transfer is cancelled and user that initiated it receives all the funds back. It can happen with explicit rejection with use of first operation. Can also happen during block processing when either agent or to account failed to approve before ratification deadline.
