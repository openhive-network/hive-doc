netRshares

netRshares: bigint

This is a crucial number representing the total "Reward Shares" accumulated by this content from upvotes. Think of it as the post's "vote weight" or "reward influence." The higher this number, the greater its potential share of the Hive reward pool. It's not a direct dollar amount but an internal blockchain measure. The final payout is calculated based on this value relative to other content paying out at the same time.

Example

150255792948762
